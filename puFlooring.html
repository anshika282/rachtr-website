PU FLOORING

HOME > INDUSTRIAL FLOORING

Polyurethane (PU) coating is essentially a type of plastic material or polymer that is formed by combining polyols with diisocyanates through the use of necessary catalysts or additives.

Polyurethane coating is an excellent way to add strength and beauty to the floor of our homes, offices, and manufacturing plants.

Polyurethane coating is an excellent solution for floors that need to be waterproof, stain-resistant, or slip-resistant.

VARIANTS OF PU FLOORING
  • Normal PU Flooring
  • PU Concrete
  • Poly Urea Flooring

PU CONCRETE

PU-Concrete, a blend of polyurethane and cement, offers exceptional strength, versatility, and waterproof properties. Ideal for various industries and high-traffic areas, it combines hardness and flexibility to resist corrosion, abrasion, and UV rays. Upgrade your floors with this perfect solution for improved appearance and quality.

POLYUREA FLOORING

Polyurea, a subset of polyurethanes, functions similarly to PU coating on concrete floors. Formed by an amine-isocyanate reaction, it offers superior resistance to harsh environments and easy curing. While slightly more expensive than polyurethane, it's ideal for construction, bridges, car parks, and roof repairs. Though it may lose gloss over time, its easy installation makes it a popular choice.
